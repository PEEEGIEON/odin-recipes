Crepe with fillings

CrÃªpes are very thin pancakes.
They can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings.
The breakfast staple dates back to at least 13th-century France.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the milk, water, eggs, and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in the flour and butter.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.
